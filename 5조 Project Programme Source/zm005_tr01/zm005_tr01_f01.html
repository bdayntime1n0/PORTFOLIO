FORM MULTI_QC .

  LOOP AT GT_DISP ASSIGNING FIELD-SYMBOL(<FS_DISP>).

    PERFORM QUANTITY_CHECK CHANGING <FS_DISP>.

  ENDLOOP.

  REFRESH GT_DISP.
  PERFORM GET_DATA.
  PERFORM TABLE_REFRESH.


ENDFORM.

FORM UCOMM_REQC .

  DATA: LT_SELECTED_ROWS TYPE LVC_T_ROID.

  CALL METHOD GO_ALV->GET_SELECTED_ROWS
    IMPORTING
      ET_ROW_NO = LT_SELECTED_ROWS.                 " Numeric IDs of Selected Rows
  .
  IF LT_SELECTED_ROWS IS INITIAL.             "###### ###"
    MESSAGE I001(00) WITH '### #####.'.
  ENDIF.

  LOOP AT LT_SELECTED_ROWS INTO DATA(LS_SELECTED_ROW).

    READ TABLE GT_DISP ASSIGNING FIELD-SYMBOL(<FS_DISP>) INDEX LS_SELECTED_ROW-ROW_ID.
    CHECK SY-SUBRC EQ 0.

    PERFORM RE_CHECK CHANGING <FS_DISP>.

  ENDLOOP.

  REFRESH GT_DISP.
  PERFORM GET_DATA.
  PERFORM TABLE_REFRESH.

ENDFORM.
