FORM SELECTED_DATA_RTN .

  REFRESH GT_DISPLAY.

  SELECT C~CARRID,
         A~CARRNAME,
         B~CONNID,
         C~FLDATE,
         B~COUNTRYFR,
         B~COUNTRYTO,
         C~SEATSMAX_F,  " 최대좌석 First
         C~SEATSMAX_B,  " 최대좌석 Business
         C~SEATSMAX,    " 최대좌석 Economy
         C~SEATSOCC_F,  " 현재예약된 First
         C~SEATSOCC_B,  " 현재예약된 Business
         C~SEATSOCC,    " 현재예약된 Economy
         C~PRICE,       " 운임
         C~CURRENCY     " 통화
    FROM SCARR AS A INNER JOIN SPFLI   AS B  ON B~CARRID EQ A~CARRID
                    INNER JOIN SFLIGHT AS C  ON C~CARRID EQ B~CARRID
                                            AND C~CONNID EQ B~CONNID

   " Selection Screen 에서 검색조건들을 누락없이 적용한다.
   WHERE C~CARRID IN @S_CARRID
     AND C~CONNID IN @S_CONNID
     AND C~FLDATE IN @S_FLDATE

    INTO TABLE @GT_DISPLAY.

  IF SY-SUBRC EQ 0.
    SORT GT_DISPLAY BY CARRID
                       CONNID
                       FLDATE.
  ENDIF.

ENDFORM.
